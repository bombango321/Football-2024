About Football

Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is the world's most popular sport. It is played between two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball. The objective is to score by getting the ball into the opposing goal. The sport is known for its simplicity and universal appeal.

Football is governed by the Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA), which organizes the World Cup every four years. The game has various formats and competitions across the globe, including domestic leagues, continental championships, and international tournaments.

Famous Footballers

Some of the most famous footballers in history include Pelé, Diego Maradona, Lionel Messi, and Cristiano Ronaldo. These players have become legends due to their extraordinary skills, achievements, and contributions to the sport.

Football Rules

The basic rules of football include:

  • Two teams of 11 players each.
  • The match is played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end.
  • The ball can be played with any part of the body except the hands and arms (goalkeepers can use their hands within the penalty area).
  • A goal is scored when the ball completely crosses the goal line between the goalposts.
  • The team with the most goals at the end of the match wins.
